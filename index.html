The premise

Readiness is more than how rested you feel.

Sleep and recovery trackers infer readiness from passive body signals measured overnight. NeuroReadiness instead actively checks present cognitive performance with brief tasks, then combines it with live physiology — so the reading reflects how you are right now, not how last night went.

01

Check current performance

A few minutes of reaction-time, inhibition and memory tasks measure where your attention and control actually sit today.

02

Understand what may be limiting it

Pulse and motion signals add physiological context, so a dip reads as load or fatigue rather than an unexplained number.

03

Track changes against your own baseline

Every result is expressed relative to your recent normal — the only honest frame for a personal performance signal.

Science & transparency

Built to show its reasoning.

PVT

Psychomotor Vigilance Task

A sustained-attention reaction test — the gold-standard measure of how alert and vigilant you are right now.

Stroop

Colour-word interference

Name the ink colour, not the word. Measures cognitive control — your ability to inhibit an automatic response.

2-back

Working-memory updating

Track whether the current item matches the one two steps back. Indexes how much you can hold and update at once.

The cognitive paradigms are validated. The 0–100 scores are transparent heuristics meant for comparison against your own sessions — not population diagnosis.
